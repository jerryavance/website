Jerry Vance Anguzu

I am Jerry Vance, a Data Scientist and Software Engineer. I am interested in the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and optimization, focusing primarily on the design and analysis of statistical methods for high-dimensional problems in the context of learning from data. My focus is to build intelligent systems such as self-driving cars using large-scale, real-world data that have real-world impact. I also have interest in computer vision and machine learning.

I did my Certificate Program in Computer and Data Science from Massachusetts Institute of Technology under the MIT ReACT program. I did my bachelors of Science in Statistics from Kyambogo University. In my current research, I work on combining probabilistic graphical modeling and deep learning to design models for structured high-dimensional data such as text. I also work on web development and have held various internship roles at several companies.
